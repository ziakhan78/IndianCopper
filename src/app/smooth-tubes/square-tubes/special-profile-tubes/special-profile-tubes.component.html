Tube Tech manufactures Copper Special Profile Tubes and Brass Special Profile Tubes with cross-sections of any shape, according to the customer’s specifications. They are many-purpose products which can be ordered according to a respective standard, specification or tailored as to suit specific needs of customers. Our facilities are capable of producing almost any shape – which is possible through drawing operation such as: elliptical, D shaped, Oval Shaped, any customized shape for special requirements etc. – for our esteemed customers. Mechanical properties and chemical compositions are in accordance with ASTM or other common standards, as well as special customer’s requirements.

Dimensional Range

  1. Outer Diameter - 6.00 mm to 50.00 mm
  2. Wall Thickness - 0.7 mm and above
  3. Available in lengths up to 6 mtrs.
  4. Available in a variety of tempers as per the requirements.
  5. ASTM, BS, JIS, IS, DIN and all other common specifications.
