Tube Tech produces Copper Hexagonal Tubes & Brass Hexagonal Tubes for a variety of applications. These Hexagonal Copper Tubes and Hexagonal Brass Tubes are available in outer diameters 6 mm to 50 mm with wall thickness combination 0.5 mm and above. These tubes are available in light drawn, hard drawn & soft annealed tempers depending upon the requirement. Hexagonal Copper Tubes are available in both commercial grade (DHP Grade) & Electrolytic grade (ETP Grade) and Hexagonal Brass Tubes in CuZn37 grade & all other commonly used grades to suit the intended applications.

Product Features:

  1. Outer Diameter - 6.00 mm to 50.00 mm
  2. Wall Thickness - 0.7 mm and above
  3. Available in lengths up to 6 mtrs
  4. Available in a variety of tempers as per the requirements.
  5. ASTM, BS, JIS, IS, DIN and all other common specifications.
