Brass is an alloy of copper with main alloying element as Zinc. Strength, hardness, ductility, good corrosion resistance, machinability, formability and conductivity are the main characteristics of the brass tubes coupled with toughness retained above and below ambient temperature, good spark resistance and low magnetic permeability.
