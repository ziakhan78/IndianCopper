With a reputation of product excellence and reliability, Tube Tech offers its customers a wide range of high quality, soft annealed, single/double layer, plain and plastic coated copper coils for diverse applications. These tubes are manufactured with an outside diameter tolerance of +0.0/-0.08mm (0.003”), annealed at approximately 600°C, giving a hardness range of 45/60 HV5. Our smooth bore copper tubes are characterized by their bright, clean and dry internal and external surfaces. Superior dimensional control offers a trouble free performance and cost saving. The tube ends are capped at both ends to maintain the standard of cleanliness.

Tube Tech manufactures high quality plastic coated copper coils designed for protection against aggressive environment and colour coded to aid identification.
