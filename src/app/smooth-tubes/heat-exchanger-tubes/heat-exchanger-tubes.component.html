Tube Tech is a leading manufacturer and supplier of Plain & Finned heat exchanger Tubes. We manufacture and supply heat exchanger tubes in Copper, Brass, Cupro Nickel, Stainless Steel and Carbon Steel tube materials. Our on-site u-bending facility allows us to provide quick turnaround of these materials if u-bending is required. We can also fabricate U Tubes for heat exchanger applications in Stainless Steel & Carbon Steel tube materials. Our commitment to on-time delivery and supply of high-quality material has enabled us to build lasting relationships with engineering companies and end users right across the globe.

Tube Tech fabricates various Custom Engineered tubes for heat exchanger applications which include: Integral low fin tubes, Corrugated Fin Tubes, Condenser Fin Tubes, Turbo Chill Fin Tubes, End Cross Fin Tubes, Internally Enhanced Fin Tubes, U Tubes etc. These engineered tubes offer distinct advantages over traditional condenser tubes. By optimizing the heat transfer process engineered tubes increase efficiency. This allows for greater flexibility when designing new heat exchangers and also increases the performance of existing units re-tubed with engineered tubes.

Plain Heat Exchanger Tubes are available in outer diameters 9.52 mm to 75 mm while Finned Heat Exchanger Tubes are available in outer diameters 9.52 mm to 42 mm with wall thickness 0.6 mm and above.
