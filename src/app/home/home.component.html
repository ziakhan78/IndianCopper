Tube Tech Copper & Alloys Pvt. Ltd.

Tube Tech is a Customer focused, innovative and value driven company which manufactures all types of Copper and Copper alloy smooth surface tubes (plain tubes), extended surface tubes (Finned Tubes) and a vast variety of tubular products for heat transfer solutions.

Today, we have an unrivalled reputation for building robust, intelligent tubular products for all industrial and commercial heating and cooling applications, transmission systems, marine applications & thermal solutions. But our goal is to be more than just successful. It is to be admired for our culture and for the quality, service and management standards that implies. Nothing else will do!
